The Stories of Our Pets

Totoro

Totoro was meant to be my graduation gift, but we ended up getting him early. He is an extremely cuddly, soft, and social cat who loves to meow and shout for what he wants. He's not afraid of anything, not even the dogs.

Yuna

Mei's old foster parent reached out to us. They knew we had a front right leg tripawd (three-legged dog) and figured we would want another. Yuna is dumb, brave, and incredibly hungry for love and words of affirmation. If you're offering pets she's there.
